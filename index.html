ABOUT THE BAND

The band was formed in 2012 when the trio met at KNT during a play on cancer awareness. After the show, Kenneth played soundtracks on his guitar and after finding themselves idle, voila, H_art the Band was born. H_ART THE BAND has a total of 19 Song Lyrics and Featurings on Afrika Lyrics. Originated from Kenya. Genre : Rap, Afrobeats, Afro-soul, Dancehall, Gospel, Afropop, Genge, afrobeat. Follow H_ART THE BAND on the social networks Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and Youtube to suppport them.

WHY H_ART THE BAND?

H_ART THE BAND is very artistic band, and it gives me the inspiration behind their unorthodox name. The combination of poet, guitarist and the singer gives me a sense of love to the band, and for sure after their first release of nikikutazama Song, I felt in love with the Band. From then, it became my best.
